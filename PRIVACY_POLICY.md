# Privacy Policy — Budget Saver

**Effective Date:** May 13, 2026
**Last Updated:** May 13, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to **Budget Saver**. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your information is handled. This Privacy Policy explains what data Budget Saver collects, how it is used, and the choices you have regarding your information.

Please read this policy carefully. By using Budget Saver, you agree to the practices described below.

---

## Who We Are

Budget Saver is a personal finance application designed to help individuals track spending, manage budgets, and reach savings goals. The app is developed and maintained by the Budget Saver team ("we," "us," or "our").

If you have any questions about this policy, you may contact us at:

**Email:** support@budgetsaverapp.com

---

## Information We Collect

Budget Saver is designed with your privacy as a priority. The vast majority of your data never leaves your device.

### Information You Provide

All financial data you enter into Budget Saver — including transactions, budget categories, savings goals, and linked card details — is stored **locally on your device only** using your device's secure on-device storage. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

| Data Type | Where It Is Stored | Shared With Third Parties? |
|---|---|---|
| Transactions (income/expenses) | On-device only | No |
| Budget categories and limits | On-device only | No |
| Savings goals and contributions | On-device only | No |
| Linked card names and last 4 digits | On-device only | No |
| Card balances you enter manually | On-device only | No |
| Subscription tier preference | On-device only | No |

### Information Collected Automatically

Budget Saver does not automatically collect analytics, crash reports, advertising identifiers, or usage statistics. We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs (such as Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, or similar services).

If you contact us for support via email, we will receive the information you include in that communication (such as your name and email address). This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with any third party.

---

## How We Use Your Information

Because all financial data is stored locally on your device, we do not have access to it and therefore do not use it in any way. The only information we may receive is what you voluntarily send us through direct communication (e.g., a support email), which we use exclusively to assist you.

---

## Data Storage and Security

Your financial data is stored on your device using AsyncStorage, a secure local storage system provided by the React Native framework. We strongly recommend that you:

- Keep your device's operating system and Budget Saver app updated to the latest version.
- Use your device's built-in screen lock (PIN, password, Face ID, or fingerprint) to prevent unauthorized access.
- Be cautious when using Budget Saver on shared or public devices.

We do not have the ability to access, retrieve, or recover data stored on your device. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

---

## Subscription and Payments

Budget Saver offers an optional "pay what you want" subscription for premium features. At this time, subscription preferences are stored locally on your device. No payment processing is handled directly within the app. Any future integration with payment processors (such as Google Play Billing or Apple In-App Purchases) will be governed by the respective platform's privacy policy and terms of service, and this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

## Children's Privacy

Budget Saver is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through our app, please contact us immediately and we will take appropriate steps to address the situation.

---

## Third-Party Services

Budget Saver does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or data brokers. The app does not contain advertisements and does not share your data with any external parties.

---

## Your Rights and Choices

Because your data is stored entirely on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You may:

- **Access your data** at any time by opening the app.
- **Delete your data** at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.
- **Export your data** using any export features available within the app.

Residents of certain jurisdictions (such as the European Union under GDPR, or California under CCPA) may have additional rights. Since we do not collect or process your personal financial data on our servers, most of these rights are exercised directly through your device. For any other privacy-related requests, please contact us at the email address above.
