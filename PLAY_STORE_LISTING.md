---

Take control of your finances with Budget Saver — the simple, private budgeting app that helps you spend smarter and save more every month.

**Track Every Dollar**
Log your income and expenses in seconds. Choose from dozens of categories, tag transactions to a specific card or account, and set transactions to repeat weekly or monthly so you never have to log the same thing twice.

**Build Budgets That Work**
Set monthly spending limits for every category — groceries, dining, entertainment, and more. Color-coded progress bars show you exactly how close you are to your limit, so you always know when to pump the brakes.

**Reach Your Savings Goals**
Whether you're saving for a vacation, an emergency fund, or a new car, Budget Saver helps you set a target, track your progress, and add contributions whenever you're ready. Watch your goals come to life with visual progress rings.

**See Where Your Money Really Goes**
The Insights tab shows beautiful pie and bar charts of your monthly spending by category. Switch between months to spot trends, compare income vs. expenses, and see exactly how much you saved.

**Never Miss a Bill**
Add your recurring bills — rent, utilities, subscriptions — with a due date and a monthly amount. Budget Saver shows you which bills are coming up and keeps a running total of your monthly obligations.

**Manage All Your Cards in One Place**
Add your checking accounts, savings accounts, credit cards, and cash wallets. See your balances at a glance on the dashboard and tag transactions to the right card automatically.

**Export Your Data Anytime**
Download a full CSV export of your transaction history and share it via email, iCloud, Google Drive, or any app on your phone. Your data always belongs to you.

**100% Private — No Account Required**
Everything is stored locally on your device. No sign-up, no cloud sync, no ads, and no data ever leaves your phone. Budget Saver is built on the belief that your financial life is nobody's business but yours.

**Optional Support Subscription**
Budget Saver is free to use. If you find it valuable, you can choose to support development with a small monthly contribution — starting at just $3/month. Pay what feels right to you, cancel anytime.

## Privacy Policy URL

> Google Play **requires** a privacy policy URL for any app in the Finance category. Host the `PRIVACY_POLICY.md` file online before submitting. Recommended free options:

1. **GitHub Gist** — Go to [gist.github.com](https://gist.github.com), paste the privacy policy text, set it to Public, and use the Gist URL.
2. **Google Sites** — Go to [sites.google.com](https://sites.google.com), create a free page, paste the text, publish it, and use the public URL.
3. **Notion** — Create a public Notion page and share the link.

## Submission Checklist

Use this checklist when submitting in the [Google Play Console](https://play.google.com/console):

| Step | Task | Status |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Create Google Play Developer account ($25 one-time fee) | ☐ |
| 2 | Create a new app in Play Console | ☐ |
| 3 | Upload the `.aab` file (generated via the Publish button) | ☐ |
| 4 | Fill in App Title, Short Description, Full Description | ☐ |
| 5 | Upload app icon (512×512 PNG) | ☐ |
| 6 | Upload feature graphic (1024×500 PNG) | ☐ |
| 7 | Upload at least 2 screenshots | ☐ |
| 8 | Set Category to "Finance" | ☐ |
| 9 | Complete Content Rating Questionnaire | ☐ |
| 10 | Add Privacy Policy URL | ☐ |
| 11 | Set pricing to "Free" | ☐ |
| 12 | Select countries for distribution (or "All countries") | ☐ |
| 13 | Submit for review | ☐ |

> Google typically reviews new apps within **3–7 business days**. You will receive an email when the review is complete.
